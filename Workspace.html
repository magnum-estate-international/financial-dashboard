• 🆕 Shareholder Capital Deposit (June 2026): all shareholder money received by PT Magnum Estate International in June 2026 totals IDR 1.223.194.132 — Stanislav Sadovnikov IDR 661.597.066 (Capital Contribution 561.597.066 on 12 Jun + Refund Other Receivable 2 × 50.000.000 on 8 Jun) and Igor Maksimov IDR 561.597.066 (Capital Contribution, 12 Jun). These deposits carry their own dedicated category (Capital deposit / Shareholder Capital Deposit) and are classified as a financing inflow — separate from inter-company loans, loan repayments and receivables. To avoid double-counting, these capital deposits are NOT included in the "Financing Inflows" figure; they are shown as their own card up front and still count towards Net Financing & Net Cash Movement. This differs from payments to Stanislav/Igor (receivables & shareholder payments), which remain classified as financing out.

• Basis & group structure: This dashboard presents four business groups and three standalone entities: ICG Group (6 entities), PT Sky Stars Villas (3 entities: Hospitality, Building Management, Developer), KSO TUS — The Umalas Signature (2 entities: KSO SUP and KSO Magnum), PT Samahita Umalas Prasada (3 entities: PT SUP Developer, PT SUP Hospitality — The Umalas Signature operations, and PT SUP BM), plus three independent legal entities that stand on their own: PT Magnum Estate International, PT Berawa Bali Utama, and PT Umalas Lounge Rooftop. Figures are derived from the May 2026 bank reports of all groups plus the entity dashboards consolidated earlier (PT Indonesia Capital Sanur, PT Berawa Bali Utama, PT Umalas Lounge Rooftop) and the KSO The Umalas Signature cash flow report (Bank Danamon). Inter-bank "PB In to Out" / overbooking transfers within the same entity are excluded (contra entries). Loan disbursements, loan repayments, loan collections, receivable disbursements, shareholder payments, shareholder capital deposits, and the PBG licensing fee are classified as financing / non-operational — not revenue or expense.
